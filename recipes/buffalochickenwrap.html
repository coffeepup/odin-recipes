Buffalo Chicken Wrap

Description

This take on a Buffalo chicken wrap creates a bit more work breading the chicken, but the crispy texture to pair with the classic hot sauce is worth the effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast, sliced into one-inch pieces
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil, for frying
  • 1 egg
  • All-purpose flour, for breading
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • Tortilla shells
  • Diced tomato
  • Shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp Frank's Red Hot sauce
  • Blue cheese dressing

Steps

  1. Crack egg into a bowl and whisk yolk and white together
  2. Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper
  3. Bread chicken pieces by dredging in flour, dipping in egg, then dredging in Panko crumbs
  4. In a non-stick pan, fry breaded chicken pieces in oil until cooked to at least 165 F and exterior is golden brown
  5. Melt butter and add hot sauce, whisk to combine
  6. Toss cooked chicken pieces in hot sauce/butter mixture
  7. Assemble by placing shredded lettuce on middle of tortilla shell. Drizzle with Blue cheese dressing. Top with chicken pieces and diced tomatoes, wrap and serve
